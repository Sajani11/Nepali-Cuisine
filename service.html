*****Explore some of our most popular and traditional recipes*****

  1. Dal Bhat (Lentil Soup & Rice)

    1. Ingredients:
      2. - 1 cup yellow lentils (masoor dal), 1 cup basmati rice ,Ghee, cumin, turmeric, coriander, salt, water
    2. Instructions:
      1. Cook lentils with water until soft,then season with ghee, cumin, turmeric, and salt.
      2. Cook rice with water and salt until tender.
      3. Serve dal over rice with pickles and curry.



  2. Dhido (Buckwheat Porridge)

    1. Ingredients:
      2. -1 cup buckwheat flour ,Water, salt
    2. Instructions:
      1. Boil water with a pinch of salt.
      2. Gradually add buckwheat flour, stirring constantly to avoid lumps.
      3. Cook until it thickens, then serve with lentils or vegetables.



  3. Sel Roti (Rice Doughnut)

    1. Ingredients:
      2. -cups rice flour, - 1 cup sugar, - 1/2 tsp cardamom powder, - Water, ghee or oil for frying
    2. Instructions
      1. Mix rice flour, sugar, and cardamom, then add water to form a thick batter.
      2. Heat oil, shape the batter into rings, and fry until golden brown.
      3. Serve as a sweet treat during festivals.



  4. Momo (Dumplings)

    1. Ingredients:
      2. - Dumpling wrappers, Filling (ground meat or vegetables, garlic, ginger, onions, soy sauce, spices)
    2. Instructions:
      1. Mix the filling ingredients.
      2. Fill wrappers and fold into dumpling shapes.
      3. Steam for 10-12 minutes, serve with dipping sauce.
